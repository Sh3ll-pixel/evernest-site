Legal & Policies

Clear and fair policies

We believe in full transparency. Here's exactly how EverNest works.

🔄 Cancellation & Refund Policy

You may cancel your EverNest Pro subscription at any time from within the app or by contacting us at [email protected].

  • Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period.
  • You retain Pro access until your paid period expires.
  • We offer a full refund within 3 days of your initial subscription if you are unsatisfied.
  • Refunds after 3 days are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
  • To request a refund, email [email protected] with your account email and reason.

🔒 Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. EverNest collects only what is necessary to operate the service.

  • We collect your name, email, and encrypted password to create your account.
  • Bank data is accessed via Plaid and is never stored on EverNest servers beyond access tokens.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • Payment processing is handled by Stripe and PayPal — EverNest never stores card details.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
  • EverNest does not respond to request for information from state sponsored agencies.

📋 Terms of Service

  • EverNest is a personal finance and household management tool intended for personal use.
  • You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials.
  • EverNest is not a licensed financial advisor. Information displayed is for personal tracking only.
  • We reserve the right to suspend accounts that violate these terms.
  • Service availability is provided on a best-effort basis. We are not liable for data loss due to unforeseen outages.
  • These terms may be updated with 30 days notice via email.

🏦 Financial Data & Security

  • Bank connections are powered by Plaid, a trusted financial data platform used by thousands of apps.
  • EverNest uses read-only access to your bank data — we cannot move or modify funds.
  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt encryption.
  • You can disconnect your bank accounts at any time from the Financial tab.
  • EverNest complies with applicable data protection regulations.
